Pro Study Tip: Active Recall

Don't just read notes. Close the book and try to write down everything you remember. This "Active Recall" technique is scientifically proven to be 300% more effective than passive reading for long-term memory.

Understanding Negative Marking

In exams like JEE and CDS, negative marking can ruin your rank. Never guess on more than 3-4 questions. Use the process of elimination to increase your odds if you can narrow down two options.

Maintaining Mental Health

Exam preparation is a marathon. Ensure you get 7 hours of sleep and 20 minutes of physical activity daily. A fresh mind solves complex problems faster than a tired one.
